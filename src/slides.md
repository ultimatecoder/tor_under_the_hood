## Tor Relays

* Entry node (Guard node)

  * Remains for 1-2 months

  * Knows who is requesting

  * Doesn't know who requested

* Middle node

* Exit node

  * The one who actually resolves the request

  * Doesn't know who requested

  * Knows what is requested
